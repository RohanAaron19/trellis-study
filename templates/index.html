TRELLIS 3D Model Quality Study

Hello,

If you choose to participate in this study, you will view short videos of computer-generated 3D objects and indicate which ones you find to be the highest quality. The study involves:

  • Viewing short rotating-view videos of 3D models generated by an AI pipeline
  • Completing 19 preference trials, where each trial asks you to select which model you find to be the best for a given description.

The entire study should take approximately 10 to 15 minutes to complete. You will not receive any compensation for participating.

This study is conducted by researchers at Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University (Virginia Tech), and contributes to ongoing research on AI-based 3D content generation. Participation is entirely voluntary, and you may stop and close the browser window at any time without penalty. The researchers will not collect your name, email, or any other directly identifying information; all responses are anonymous click-based selections stored only with anonymous study metadata.

If you have any questions about the study, please email the Principal Investigator, Prof. Simon Stepputtis, at stepputtis@vt.edu. If you have questions about your rights as a research participant, you may contact the Virginia Tech Institutional Review Board at irb@vt.edu.

Virginia Tech determined that this project does not meet the federal definition of human subjects research.
