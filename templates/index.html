TRELLIS 3D Model Quality Study

Welcome! In this study you will be shown short videos of 3D models and asked to make simple preference selections. No prior knowledge of 3D modeling or AI is required.

The study has 19 objects and two quick decisions per object. It should take roughly 10–15 minutes.

Consent & Participation

Your responses are anonymous and will only be used for research purposes at Virginia Tech.

Virginia Tech determined that this project does not meet the federal definition of human subjects research.
